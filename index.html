Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bloom & Grow began as a small neighborhood plant shop with a passion for bringing greenery into urban spaces. What started as a humble storefront has blossomed into a beloved destination for plant enthusiasts across the city.

We carefully source our plants from trusted growers who prioritize sustainable practices. Our team of plant experts is always available to help you select the perfect plant and provide care advice to ensure your new green friend thrives.

Fast Growth

We've grown from a single store to 5 locations across the city in just 5 years.
